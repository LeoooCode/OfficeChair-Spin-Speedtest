How the speedtest works

  1. Sit down in an office chair (also works if you are not in an office) with your phone.
  2. Hold your phone in parallel to the ground with the display facing up.
  3. Press the "START" button.
  4. A 3-second countdown starts after which the 10-secound measurment will begin.
  5. Spin as fast as you can.
  6. Your average RPM (=rotations per minute) is displayed on the screen.
  7. Check with your colloges who fast they can go.
